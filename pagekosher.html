The Opportunity

The Kosher Wander is an informative travel blog that provides recipes and guidelines on how to maintain a strict diet while traveling (Kosher, vegetarian, gluten-free, etc). From her stories, recipes, and nuggets of advice, the author hopes to show that traveling to remote places should not require you to compromise on a restricted diet.

The Solution

Build a website!

The Kosher Wanderer needed a site that was not only dynamic, but responsive and user-friendly. Because the author wanted to reach a wide audience, it was important to produce useful content with lots of colour. This project was complex because the site was to have two messages; 1) travel blog and 2) recipes. After several meetings of white-boarding, I began outlining the User Flow.
